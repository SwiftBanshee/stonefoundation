REMPEL MASONRY: CERTIFIED FOUNDATION REPAIR CONTRACTORS IN ST. CATHARINES

The importance of a strong, durable foundation cannot be stressed enough. It is considered to be the most crucial part of the building as the overall structure of the building depends on the foundation, and it deserves the highest focus and attention to detail. Cracks in walls, uneven or sloping roofs, bowing walls and other such problems are signs that your foundation requires to be repaired. Rempel Foundation Solutions specializes in structural foundation repairs for residential, commercial and industrial applications. With over 20 years of experience, our team in St. Catharines will inspect your building for damages and provide appropriate solutions. We handle existing as well as new construction projects.

Why Choose Us?

Licensed, Red Seal-certified and insured workers
Certified CHANCE® helical pier foundation system installer
30-year CHANCE® product warranty
20-year warranty on waterproofing
100% satisfaction guaranteed

Chance™ Helical Pier Installation

Stabilize, support, and lift areas of a foundation that have failed.

Foundation Structural Repair

Effective reconstruction or reinforcement of exterior foundation walls.

Basement Waterproofing

Protect, drain, and grade to keep water away from structure.

Expert Masonry Restoration

Repointing, replacement, and repair of mortar joints to brick or stone exterior walls.

Commercial and Industrial Projects

Large-scale repair and restoration projects of Niagara’s commercial and community facilities.

Free Estimates

Our experienced team will help you understand any issues with your structure’s foundation and provide sound advice so you can take the right step towards securing your property. Don’t hesitate to reach out to us. We provide free estimates for our services.
